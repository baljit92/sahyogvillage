Our Centres

Sahyog Khunti

ADDRESS: VILLAGE DUGDUGIA (HUTAR)

Sahyog Village is situated outside the city of Ranchi, Jharkhand. Inaugurated in March 2008, this is a community of twenty free standing cottages fostering care and serving as children and women rehabilitation centers, a computer lab, prayer hall, sector/cricket field, playground and organic garden.

Our programs at Sahyog Khunti include:

  • Child Care Institution for Boys (50)
  • Nari Niketan (Women Home for Destitute)
  • Autism Center for Children
  • Child Line Services 1098
  • Rupantaran (A Project to Import Training in Tailoring for Women Inmates in Khunti Jail
  • Rabinder Nath Neogi Health Sub Centre.
  • Destitute Old Age Home for Women (Project of MCKS Food for Hungry Foundation Delhi)
  • Computer Laboratory for Imparting Computer Education
  • Old Age Home for Men (Khunti Town) Govt of Jharkhand Project
  • Free Cooked food at Khunti District Hospital

Sahyog Simdega

ADDRESS 1: MATRAMETA, SIMDEGA

ADDRESS 2: SAMTOLI (NEAR EVM WAREHOUSE), SIMDEGA

Sahyog Village Simdega is situated at Matrameta Village in Simdega Distt. It is 3 KM from the Main Town. Sahyog Village has its own building at Matrameta and three leased accommodation by the District Administration Simdega, Jharkhand for housing Children (Boys, Girls and Special Adoption Center).

Our programs at Sahyog Simdega include:

  • Children Home for Girls with A Capacity to Accommodate 50 Children
  • Children Home for Boys with A Capacity to Accommodate 50 Children
  • Short Stay Shelter for Trafficked Women and Children
  • Specialized Adoption Agency (SAA)
  • Free cooked food at District Hospital, Simdega

Sahyog Bokaro (Chas)

ADDRESS: Near Observation Home for Boys, Chas, Bokaro

Sahyog Bokaro was inaugrated in the year 2018 and serves as an exclusive institutional care center for boys. The home, first-of-its-kind in Distt Bokaro, presently houses 50 boys upto the age of 18 years. The Home is majorly funded by Govt. Grant under Integrated Child Protection Scheme (ICPS) Program.

Our programs at Sahyog Bokaro include:

  • Children Home for Boys with A Capacity to Accommodate 50 Children.

Sahyog Ranchi

ADDRESS: House No 416/B Road No 5/B Ashok Nagar, Ranchi - 834002

Sahyog Ranchi has been accorded license to run a MODEL SPECIALISED ADOPTION CENTER IN RANCHI CITY. The project was initiated in September 2017 and has been successfully facilitating adopting a baby and children both within and outside of India. Since its inception, the project has placed and continues to place multiple children into loving adoptive families across the country and abroad. The facility is run by member contributions and Govt Grant under ICPS Program.

Our programs at Sahyog Ranchi include:

  • Specialized Adoption Agency (SAA)

Sahyog Sundergarh

ADDRESS: Brahamanmara (behind Tehsil Office) AT/PO: Birmitrapur Distt - Sundergarh

Sahyog Birmitrapur was our first step towards the serving the Tribal areas. Originally opened in 2004, Birmitrapur is a home to 50 children who has been with us ever since they were infants. The Home is spread across one acre land and has separate buildings for boys and girls. The home is majorly funded by the Member's contribution, Nicole Reiss Wenhardt (Austria) and other sponsors.

Our programs at Sahyog Sundergarh include:

  • Children Home with A Capacity to Accommodate 60 Children
  • Computer Laboratory for Imparting Computer Education
  • Destitute Women Home

Sahyog East Singhbhum

ADDRESS: D/13 KHARKAI ENCLAVE SONARI, JAMSHEDPUR - 831011

Sahyog Jamshedpur is a specialised adoption center in Distt. Jamshedpur. The facility runs at a rented accomodation and houses more than 30 infants. The facility is run by member contributions and Govt Grant under ICPS Program.

Our programs at Sahyog East Singhbhum include:

  • Specialized Adoption Agency (SAA).

About Sahyog Village

Established in the year 2004, Sahyog Village is registered under the Societies Regd. Act XXI of 1860 as an All-India Society. We focuse on child rehabilitation, specialized adoption centers, fostering care for the elderly, and women's welfare programs.
