Covid-19 Relief Distribution


First Distribution - 350 Food Packets were distributed to villagers at Block Arki-Birbanki Panchayat & Kochang in association with our partner MCKS Food For The Hungry Foundation, Delhi. Each Packet consisted of 10Kg Rice, 3Kg Dal, 3Kg Jaggery, 2 Kg Mustard Oil, 1 Kg Spices, Biscuit and other food items along with Bathing & Clothes Washing Soap. The distribution was coordinated with #childlinekhunti team.


Second Distribution - We thank our donors Mantej Pardesi & Esther Simmendinger for their contribution towards COVID-19 Relief work in Jharkhand. With their help, we distributed packets consisting of Rice, Dal, Cooking Oil, Potatoes & Onions, Soap, Masks & Other Essentials to 70 families at Village Kairbera, Block Pakartanr, District Simdega


Third Distribution Sahyog Village Khunti & Ranchi Centre distributed food Items to the Migrant workers and other needy citizens at Capital Ranchi, Jharkhand.
