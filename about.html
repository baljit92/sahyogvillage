What we do
  • Child Rehabilitation and Institutional care
  • Operating Health Sub Centre for the villagers
  • Hosting Computer Labs for Computer Education for school and college going children
  • Specialized Adoption Centers at Ranchi, Jamshedpur and Simdega Jharkhand
  • Operating a Legal Aid Centre at Khunti, Jharkhand
  • Modal Specialized Adoption Agency in Ranchi, Jharkhand
  • Running five Children Homes in Khunti, Bokaro and Simdega (two) District and one at Birmitrapur, Odisha
  • Running Child Line Services 1098 at Khunti and Simdega, Jharkhand
  • Running Destitute Women Home at Khunti and Simdega
  • Running an Old Destitute Home at Khunti, Jharkhand
  • Running RUPANTARAN Program for the women inmates of Khunti Jail
  • Running D Pharma College at Khunti, Jharkhand
  • Organic farming in Khunti campus
  • Providing free cooked food at Khunti & Simdega on daily basis with the assistance of MCKS Food for Hungry Foundation

About Sahyog Village

Established in the year 2004, Sahyog Village is registered under the Societies Regd. Act XXI of 1860 as an All-India Society. We focuse on child rehabilitation, specialized adoption centers, fostering care for the elderly, legal aid, and women's welfare programs.
